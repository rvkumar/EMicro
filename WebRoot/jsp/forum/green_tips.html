TAKE CARE OF OUR EARTH
Maintain Indoor Plants

Keep plants in your home to help moderate the temperature, air quality and keep you naturally feeling cool.
Ensure the refrigerator doors are closed properly

Check to make sure your refrigerator door seals when closes so that you dont consume extra enerty to keep things cool.
Cover liquids stored in refrigerators

Do not put uncovered liquids in the refrigerator. The liquids give off vapours that add to the compressor workload
Car pool to office with friends and colleagues who live around you

The reports says vehicular pollution adds to almost 13% of green house gas emissions word wide.
Reuse, Reduce, Recycle

Pass old clothes, books, shoes, electronics and other household items to schools, orphanages and shelters in need.
