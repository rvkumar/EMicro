The first plant of Micro Labs, ML 01,with two locations, was established in the year 1982,at Hosur.

It produces Tablets, Capsules and Ointments and is approved by WHO, UNICEF, MSF, INVIMA- Columbia, Indonesia, MOH- Vietnam, IDA-Netherland, MOH - Sudan, National Drug Authority - Uganda, MOH - Kenya, NAFDAC-Nigeria, Drug Administration and Control Authority of Ethiopia, Tanzania, Sandoz, Eurodrug, ISO 14001 &18001 certification audit has been completed and Awarded with the Safety award 2008 by Tamilnadu Government.
