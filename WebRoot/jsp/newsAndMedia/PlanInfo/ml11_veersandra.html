The Veersandra plant commissioned in the year 2003. The facility is dedicated for the manufacturing of Beta lactam, Oral Dosage Forms. The dosage forms produced are Dispersible tablets, Uncoated and Coated tablets, Hard gelatin capsules and Powder For Oral Suspension/ Solution. The facility is approved by UK MHRA, TGA Australia, Health Canada, MCC South Africa, IDA and various MOH.

It supplies the products to UK, ROW countries and Domestic Market

The following are the few major products:
Pulmoxyl 250/500mg, Bactoclav Tablets, Pulmoclox Capsules
