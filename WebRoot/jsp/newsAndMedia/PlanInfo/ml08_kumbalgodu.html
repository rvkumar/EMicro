Kumbalgodu plant was commissioned in 2005 and it had been upgraded in 2010-2011 to meet the Global Regulatory Requirements.
The facility is approved by WHO. The facility is designed to handle Anti-TB products containing Rifampin combination amongst other products.

The facility is currently producing products for Domestic & ROW markets and for WHO submission.

The following projects are under process for WHO submission,
