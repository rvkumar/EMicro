The Peenya Unit 2 manufacturing facility was commissioned in the year 1978 by the Eros Pharma Management. This facility was acquired by Micro Labs Ltd in the year 2002. The facility was renamed as Micro Labs Ltd (ML 12) Peenya Unit 2 in the year 2005.

The facility has been manufacturing tablets, capsules and topicals for domestic and ROW markets.

Medicines under the category of cardiovascular drugs, anti-hypertensives, anti-diabetics, NSAIDs etc are being manufactured under the brand names such as Dolo 650, Dolo 500, Divon 50, Amlong, Avas, Melmet, Angizaar 50 and Angizaar H etc.
